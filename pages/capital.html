The Three Fs

The foundation of everything we do

Fitness

We challenge each other physically through peer-led, outdoor workouts that build strength, endurance, and mental toughness.

  • • Running and cardio
  • • Team challenges
  • • All fitness levels welcome

Fellowship

We build deep, authentic relationships through shared experiences, vulnerability, and mutual support in life's challenges.

  • • Coffee after workouts
  • • Slack community
  • • Social events
  • • Life support network

  • • Purpose-driven living
