What is F3?

F3 is free. No membership fees, no dues, no hidden costs. We believe fitness and community should be accessible to all men.

F3 is peer-led. Our workouts are designed and led by fellow PAX (participants), not professional trainers. Every man has the potential to lead.

F3 is open to all men. No matter your fitness level, age, background, or beliefs - if you're a man who wants to grow, you're welcome here.

The F3 Promise

  • Free workouts, every day
  • Peer-led, outdoor fitness
  • Fellowship and accountability
  • Character and leadership development
  • Community service opportunities

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be in shape to join?

Not at all! F3 welcomes men of all fitness levels. Workouts are scalable to your current abilities, and the community is incredibly supportive of newcomers.

What should I bring to my first workout?

Just bring yourself, a water bottle, and comfortable workout clothes. We use bodyweight exercises and simple equipment that's provided at each AO.

How often should I come?

Come as often as you'd like! Many PAX come 3-5 times per week, but even coming once a week will help you build relationships and see progress.

What if I can't do all the exercises?

That's completely normal! Every exercise has modifications, and the Q (workout leader) will help you find the right level. The goal is progress, not perfection.
